Starting your Minefort server

xDefnite

Last update: Jan 20, 2023

Starting your very own Minefort server is rather quite easy. Once your server has been created, you can follow these steps to start it up from the Minefort.com website:

  1. Log in to your Minefort account;
  2. Head to the “My Servers” page in your Minefort dashboard;
  3. Press the “Wake up server” button and wait for your server to download;
  4. Now, press the “Start server” button to get your server to start.
